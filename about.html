About ST.GEORGE Bakery

Our Story

Saint George Bakery was founded in 1935 in Port Said, Egypt, with a simple mission: to bring the finest baked goods to our community. What started as a small family operation has grown into a beloved institution, trusted by generations of customers.

Our Tradition

We believe in the art of traditional baking. Every loaf of bread, every pastry, and every cake is crafted with precision, premium ingredients, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Our recipes have been passed down through our family, maintaining the authentic flavors that our customers love.

Our Commitment

At ST.GEORGE, we are dedicated to:

  • Using only the finest, freshest ingredients
  • Maintaining traditional baking methods
  • Providing exceptional customer service
  • Supporting our local community
  • Continuously improving our craft

Why Choose Us?

With nearly 90 years of experience, ST.GEORGE Bakery stands as a beacon of quality in the baking industry. Our commitment to excellence, combined with our passion for traditional baking techniques, makes us the perfect choice for your daily bread and special occasions.
